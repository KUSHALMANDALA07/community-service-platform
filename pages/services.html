Our Core Offerings

Community Relief Services

Empowering lives through targeted assistance, relief campaigns, medical care, and educational growth.

1. Food Assistance

Daily hot meals, grain distribution, surplus food recovery from events, and emergency meal packages for vulnerable families.

Request Food Help

2. Medical Support

Doctor consultations, free medication supply, hospital admission assistance, and blood donation matching.

Open Medical Hub

3. Education Support

Student scholarship grants, free evening tuition classes, career mentoring, and digital device provision.

Open Education Hub

4. Emergency Relief

Immediate disaster response for floods, fires, cyclones, and accidents with rapid response volunteer teams.

SOS Emergency Hub

5. Clothing Donation

Collecting, washing, and distributing warm winter clothes, school uniforms, and casual attire for children and adults.

Donate Clothes

6. Book Donation

Building community libraries and distributing textbooks, storybooks, and competitive exam guides to students.

Donate Books

7. Senior Citizen Care

Home visitation, healthcare support, grocery runs, and emotional companionship for elderly citizens living alone.

Senior Support

8. Women Support

Legal assistance, skill development workshops, maternity health guidance, and emergency safety support.

Women Helpline

9. Child Welfare

Protecting child rights, nutritional support for malnourished infants, and recreational activity drives.

Child Welfare

10. Animal Rescue

Stray feeding drives, emergency veterinary medical care, anti-rabies vaccination camps, and adoption aid.

Animal Rescue
