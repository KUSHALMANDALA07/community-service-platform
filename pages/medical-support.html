Emergency & Preventive Services

Medical Relief Channels

1. Blood Donation Match

Connecting emergency patients with verified voluntary blood donors across all blood groups (A+, B+, O-, etc.).

2. Free Medicine Support

Providing essential prescription drugs and chronic care medicines for poor patients through pharmacy partners.

Request Medicines

3. Hospital Admission Aid

Helping low-income families navigate government hospital admissions and emergency ICU bed allocations.

Hospital Help

4. Doctor Tele-Consultation

Free virtual consultation with general physicians and specialists for basic diagnostic assistance.

Book Consultation

5. Ambulance SOS

Fast dispatching of free emergency ambulances for critical trauma cases and cardiac emergencies.

Call Ambulance

6. Medical Equipment Loan

Free short-term rental of oxygen concentrators, wheelchairs, hospital beds, and crutches.
